Skilled Worker Salary Checker

This tool gives an indicative comparison against Skilled Worker visa salary rules. Always verify figures on GOV.UK and seek legal advice for immigration decisions.

1. Find the occupation

About SOC codes
SOC codes classify jobs based on the main duties. For Skilled Worker visas, the code must reflect the role being sponsored. Match duties, not job titles.

2. Hours & salary

Salary rules
Skilled Worker salaries must meet the highest of:
• General salary threshold
• Occupation-specific salary ("going rate")
• Minimum hourly requirement based on contracted hours

Only guaranteed earnings count toward the salary.

3. Scenario

Understanding salary discounts
Lower salary thresholds apply only for specific applicant categories. New entrant and PhD rates apply only where eligibility criteria are met.
